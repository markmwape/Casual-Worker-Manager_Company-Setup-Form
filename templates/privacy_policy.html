At Embee Accounting, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our workforce management platform and related services.

By using our Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us for support
  • Subscribe to our services

2.2 Types of Data Collected

Account Information:

  • Name and email address

Employee Data:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our workforce management platform
  • Process attendance tracking and payroll calculations
  • Generate reports and analytics

3.2 Communication and Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

Data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols

Access Controls

Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication

Secure Infrastructure

Cloud infrastructure with regular security audits and monitoring

Data Backups

Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery procedures

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Employee Records: Retained according to local employment law requirements
  • Payroll Data: Retained for tax and audit purposes (typically 7 years)
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 12 months for security and analytics

Upon account termination, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
